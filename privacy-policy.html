1. Overview

MeetingMind ("we", "our", "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Chrome extension provides AI-powered meeting transcription, summaries, and productivity features while keeping all your data local and secure.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

What We Store Locally:

  • Meeting Transcripts: Text transcriptions of your meetings (no audio files are saved)
  • Generated Summaries: AI-generated summaries and key points from your meetings
  • Action Items: Tasks and decisions extracted from meetings
  • Meeting Metadata: Meeting titles, dates, durations, and platforms
  • User Preferences: Your settings like dark mode, language, and keyboard shortcuts
  • Meeting IQ Scores: Quality metrics for your meetings
  • Reminders: Scheduled notifications for action items

Where Data is Stored:

All data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB, a secure client-side database. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is only accessible by the MeetingMind extension
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the extension
  • Is not synced to any cloud service

What We Don't Collect:

  • ‚ùå Audio recordings (only text transcripts)
  • ‚ùå Video content
  • ‚ùå Personal information beyond what you type
  • ‚ùå Usage analytics or telemetry
  • ‚ùå Browsing history
  • ‚ùå Cookies (except Chrome's standard extension storage)
  • ‚ùå IP addresses or location data

3. Third-Party Services

Chrome Built-in AI APIs

When available and enabled, MeetingMind uses Chrome's experimental built-in AI APIs:

  • Prompt API: For generating summaries and extracting action items
  • Summarizer API: For creating concise meeting summaries
  • Writer API: For drafting follow-up emails

These APIs process data locally on your device using Google's Gemini Nano model. Your data is not sent to Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy for more information about Chrome's AI features.

Web Speech API (Fallback)

When Chrome's built-in AI is unavailable, MeetingMind uses the Web Speech API for speech recognition. This API:

  • Sends audio to Google's servers for processing
  • Returns text transcripts to your browser
  • Does not store audio on Google's servers (processed in real-time)
  • Is governed by Google's Privacy Policy

You can disable this feature in the extension settings if you prefer not to use cloud-based speech recognition.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to any third parties.

Your meeting transcripts, summaries, and action items are:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never uploaded to our servers (we don't have any!)
  • Never shared with advertisers or data brokers
  • Only accessible to you through the extension

5. Data Security

How We Protect Your Data:

  • Local Storage: All data stored in your browser's secure IndexedDB
  • No External Servers: Zero server-side infrastructure means zero server-side risks
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data stays on your device only
  • Content Security Policy (CSP): Prevents injection attacks and malicious code execution
  • Manifest V3: Uses Chrome's latest security standards
  • Minimal Permissions: Only requests necessary browser permissions
  • No External Dependencies: Zero third-party libraries that could leak data

Data Encryption:

Data in IndexedDB is protected by Chrome's built-in security mechanisms. If you enable disk encryption on your device, your meeting data will be encrypted at rest.

7. Permissions Explained

MeetingMind requests the following Chrome permissions:

Permission Why We Need It

storage Store transcripts, summaries, and settings locally in your browser

8. Children's Privacy

MeetingMind is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Users

MeetingMind is available worldwide. Since all data processing happens locally on your device, no data crosses international borders. Your data stays in the country where your device is located.
